## Customize

You can customize
<a href="/?path=/docs/form-switch-tile-specs--docs" target="_self">text and background</a>.

<p-switch-tile
    checked-background="success"
    unchecked-background="danger"
>
    <span slot="checked-text">Stencil</span>
    <span slot="unchecked-text">VueJS</span>
</p-switch-tile>

<p-switch-tile
    checked-background="warning"
    unchecked-background="secondary"
>
    <span slot="checked-text">Nest</span>
    <span slot="unchecked-text">Node</span>
</p-switch-tile>
